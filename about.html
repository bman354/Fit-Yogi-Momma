Fit Yogi Momma, Sonya Merriman, has had a passion for fitness since she was a young girl competing in gymnastics. Now as a busy mom of three, she loves to include fitness during family bonding time. Sonya received her Personal Training Certification 10 years ago. Immediately following earning her certification, she began working full-time as a physical therapist technician. During this time, her love for prehab and rehab blossomed. Soon after a personal love for yoga led Sonya to attended a 200 hour Yoga Teacher Training at North Port Yoga + Wellness. This created an even deeper passion for fitness by evolving it to include the wellness of mind, body and soul. Whether your are working with Sonya individually or in group sessions, her love of fitness and wellness is apparent. Her expertise allows her to adapt her training for any skill level. After a few sessions, you will find that Sonya's passion will become your passion too.
